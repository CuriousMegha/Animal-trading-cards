Cavy

Guinea pigs love cucumbers and eat plenty of fresh grass.

  • Family: Caviidae
  • Weight: 200 - 1500 grams
  • Body length : 6 - 16 inches
  • Litter size : 1 - 13 pups

They are fairly social and timid at first. Small , cute ,funny and curious, they make excellent pets. They come in mostly golden, brown, black, white and gray.
